Future Plan

Our ultimate aim of special education is to make the mentally challenged person independent to the maximum possible extent to lead a life of dignity and self-respect. To make them productive member of the society. We have planned to improve vocational unit with home science and other small activities in large-scale. Also improve therapy unit and on job training program.

Funding

A fee of Rs.2,000 is charged but many are given free ship and concessions since most of our students belong to the slum area lower- and middle-income group. Our annual expenses are not bearable, we depend on various individual donors and philanthropic organizations.

About Asmita Special School

About Us

Inception: Started with one student in Turbhe of Navi Mumbai on 4th June 1998
Service Provided: Day school, Vocational therapy, Parental counseling, Computer training.

Our Mission

We staff - In life are to serve the disable to develop services and programs for empowering persons with special needs and their families

Our Vision

To empower get mentally challenged with skill that enable them to lead a life of dignity and respect. Even to make them independent in maximum extent.

Our Objective

To make the person with special needs a contributing member of the community by becoming self-confident and self-reliant.

Activities

Students of the age of 3 years onwards admitted and are placed into different levels play group to vocational taking in to consideration the chronological age, IQ, teachers’ observation, individual abilities and needs. They are trained in personal skills, psychological skills, motor skills, function academics, speech and language, art & craft, yoga, music, dance, drama, swimming, sports, computer, camp and various extra-curricular activities. Students have participated and excelled in special Olympics; inter school competitions in dance, drama, music, painting etc. and achieved medals and trophies.

Staff

Special Educators, Vocational instructor, Computer instructor, Music and Drawing teacher, Doctor, Speech and Occupational Therapist.
